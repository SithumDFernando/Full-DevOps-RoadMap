# Quiz 11

1. What is the Principle of Least Privilege in IAM?
<details><summary>Answer</summary>
Granting a user, service, or role only the absolute minimum permissions necessary to perform its intended task, and nothing more.
</details>

2. If you provision a resource in AWS, who is responsible for turning it off so you don't get billed?
<details><summary>Answer</summary>
You are. The cloud is a shared responsibility model, but cost management of provisioned resources falls entirely on the customer.
</details>

3. Which AWS service is comparable to a Virtual Machine (VM)?
<details><summary>Answer</summary>
EC2 (Elastic Compute Cloud).
</details>

4. Why might an S3 bucket cost you money even if you are on the free tier?
<details><summary>Answer</summary>
The free tier has limits (e.g., 5GB storage, 20,000 GET requests). If you store huge files or your bucket goes viral and gets millions of requests, you will be billed for the overage.
</details>
